About us!

Based in Lisbon, Ana Catarina Veiga Events specializes in the design and execution of bespoke special occasions tailored to reflect the client's individual style and personality. Whether it's a large-scale, branded corporate event or an intimate gathering, Ana Catarina Veiga and her team offer invaluable expertise to ensure that your event is truly unforgettable.

Meet the Team

Events Organizer

Catarina started her career in the event industry a few years ago as a team coordinator for events across Lisbon. She is a Certified Event Organizer and has completed a Technical course in Event Organization from IEFP. With experience in producing corporate and family events, she has decided to start her own company due to her diverse experience in the event industry.
